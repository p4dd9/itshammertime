introduction

Pick up the hammer and smash the streamer's livestream window. Use this extension to relief inner stress or to create some fun screenshots with your favourite streamer

A stylish Hammer is a happy Hammer

customizeability

Every person has its own style. So do hammers! Change your hammer's particles by shape and color using the enchantment book in the game menu to the left.

Feel the Hammer, Be the Hammer

controls and input devices

The Mouse

Move your mouse over the livestream window to control the hammer. A left-click slams the hammer right into the screen.

Pro Tip: Rumors say, holding left-click unleashes your inner berserker.

The Controller (Chrome Browser only)

Get your Xbox360 controller and plug it in! Use A to smash the screen and the left-stick to navigate the hammer.
